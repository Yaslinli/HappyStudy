ABOUT-

A former cinema on the 20's, this is now one of Amsterdam's coolest coffee shops.
It screams hipster-cool but that also means they serve a mean cup of coffee.
Their excellent free Wi-Fi also means you'll spend hours working away here without being disturbed.
